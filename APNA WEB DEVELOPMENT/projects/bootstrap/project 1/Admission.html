Admission Process of Zeal College

  1. Eligibility Criteria:

    Ensure you meet the eligibility criteria for the desired course. Typically, this includes specific educational qualifications and minimum percentage requirements.

  2. Online Registration:

    Visit the official Zeal College website. Fill out the online application form with accurate personal and academic details. Create an account for future reference and communication.

  3. Submission of Documents:

    Prepare and upload required documents, which may include:

    • 10th and 12th mark sheets
    • Transfer certificate
    • Caste certificate (if applicable)
    • Aadhar card or ID proof
    • Passport-sized photographs
  4. Application Fee Payment:

    Pay the required application fee online through the available payment modes (credit/debit card, net banking, etc.).

  5. Entrance Examination:

    Some courses may require candidates to take an entrance exam. Check the specific requirements for your desired program. Prepare for the entrance exam and participate as per the schedule.

  6. Counseling Process:

    Qualified candidates will be called for a counseling session. During this session:

    • Review your preferences and select the desired course.
    • Confirm seat availability and make necessary course selections.
  7. Admission Confirmation:

    Once you secure a seat, you will receive an admission offer letter. Confirm your admission by paying the initial fee as specified in the offer letter.

  8. Document Verification:

    Attend a document verification session at the college to validate your submitted documents and ensure everything is in order.

  9. Final Enrollment:

    Complete the enrollment process by submitting any remaining documents and paying the total fees for the course. Collect your student ID card and any other necessary materials from the administration office.
